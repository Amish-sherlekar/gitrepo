once upon a time in a forest there was a monkey who resided on a berries tree, which was on the banks of the river,in the same forest there was a crocodile and his wife.
one day the crocodile came to the bank of the river and rested under the tree.the kind hearted monkey offered him some fruits crocodile came back the next day for more fruits, as he loved them. as days passed by, the crocodile and monkey became good friends.
